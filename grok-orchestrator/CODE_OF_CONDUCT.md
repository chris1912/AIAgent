# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

## Our Standard / 我们的标准

Contributors must communicate respectfully, describe technical disagreements with evidence, and keep reviews focused on the work. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and deliberate exposure of private information are not acceptable.

贡献者必须保持尊重，以证据说明技术分歧，并让评审聚焦于工作本身。骚扰、歧视、威胁、人肉搜索以及故意暴露私密信息均不可接受。

## Enforcement / 执行

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer. Reports will be reviewed in confidence when possible. A maintainer may remove abusive content, request changes, restrict participation, or close a contribution when necessary to protect the project community.

请将行为问题私下报告给仓库维护者。条件允许时，报告将被保密处理。为保护项目社区，维护者可以删除攻击性内容、要求修改、限制参与或关闭贡献。
